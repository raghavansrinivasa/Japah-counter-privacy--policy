Privacy Policy

Your Privacy is Protected

Our Privacy Commitment

At JAPAH COUNTER, we believe your privacy is fundamental. This privacy policy explains our commitment to protecting your personal information and data.

Simple Promise: We do not collect, store, track, or share any of your personal data. Your information stays on your device, always.

Information We Do NOT Collect

JAPAH COUNTER is designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect any of the following:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • App interaction data
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Any other personal or sensitive information

How the App Works

JAPAH COUNTER operates entirely on your device. All counting data, settings, and any other app-related information is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

This means:

  • Your counting data belongs to you
  • No internet connection is required for core functionality
  • Your privacy is maintained at all times

Third-Party Services

JAPAH COUNTER does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. The app operates independently without connecting to external services that might compromise your privacy.

Data Security

Since we don't collect any data, there's no risk of your information being compromised in a data breach. Your information never leaves your device, providing the highest level of security possible.

Children's Privacy

JAPAH COUNTER is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. Since we don't collect any personal information, there are no privacy concerns for younger users.

Updates to This Policy

While our core commitment to not collecting data will never change, we may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in legal requirements or app functionality. Any updates will be posted here with a new effective date.
